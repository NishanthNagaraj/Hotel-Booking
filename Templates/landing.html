    About Us

    THE BEACH BAY HOTEL SERVICES SINCE 2010 Carrying forward a glorious legacy of elegance & hospitality since the Beginning. From land to sea, The Beach Bay Hotel is luxuries abound. Focus on life’s simple pleasures with serene spa experiences or discover the allure of our underwater majesties. From deep sea diving and snorkeling or sailing, to basking in the Marina sunshine - your ultimate indian escape is exactly as you wish.
    Natural light draws you in to a space so calm, so divine, you can hear its heartbeat. Simplicity and sophistication fuse with ultra-modern amenities to create a memorable living experience. An air of understated elegance pervades these contemporarily designed rooms with its soothing colour palette that encourages relaxation. Crawl in, curl up and let room service take care of the rest.
    Glorious views of the crystal-clear waters of the Bay of Bengal Ocean come as standard in all of our five-star hotels and luxury villas. Alongside stunning seaside locations, effortlessly elegant interiors and impeccable service – including the assistance of our knowledgeable concierges – you can look forward to a stylish selection of destination bars and restaurants, luxury spas and boutiques
    Home to some of the most sought-after restaurants in the world, The River Front Hotel stimulates your palate with an array of delights. The world’s finest chefs prepare the most imaginative culinary traditions from across the world. Place yourself at the cusp of an exquisite culinary adventure when you visit The Beach Bay Hotel.
    Awaken your inner self with our award winning, all-natural spas rooted in traditional Indian well-being and spirituality. We offer a milieu of old India therapies and beauty secrets to foster harmony and equilibrium within you. These time-tested wellness rituals are now within reach inside the soothing sanctuaries of every Spa.
